Minder

Reminders for the forgetful, the distracted, and the busy

Create as many reminders as you need

Real life is full of things you need to remember, both big and small. Time to pick up your daughter from practice? Time to take your medicine? Walk the dog? Leave for work? You can store literally millions of reminders in Minder.

Create a reminder for anything

Minder is built from the ground up with versatility in mind. Do you just need your phone to beep to gently remind you it's time to leave? Minder can do that. Do you need your phone to wake you up and make sure you don't go back to sleep until you've taken your insulin? Minder can do that, too.

Minder can use any ringtone or notification sound. It can vibrate, flash, yell your name. It can get your attention even if you left your phone on silent. Or it can be as discreet as a message in your notification shade. It's up to you.

Be reminded when you need it, where you need it

Minder knows you can't wash the dishes while you're at work. Attach reminders to a GPS location or WiFi network, and Minder will wait to remind you until you get where you need to be.
